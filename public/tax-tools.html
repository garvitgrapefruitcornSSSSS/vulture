Best Tax Saving Tools

Explore the top tax-saving tools to maximize your savings and reduce your tax liabilities. Start making smart financial decisions today!

Public Provident Fund (PPF)

Interest Rate: 7.1%

Lock-in Period: 15 Years

Exemption: Section 80C

Description: PPF is a government-backed tax-saving investment with attractive interest rates and long-term returns.

Equity-Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS)

Returns: Market Linked

Lock-in Period: 3 Years

Exemption: Section 80C

Description: ELSS funds are mutual funds that offer tax benefits and have a relatively short lock-in period.

National Pension System (NPS)

Returns: 8-10% (approx.)

Lock-in Period: Until Retirement

Exemption: Section 80CCD

Description: NPS is a government initiative for retirement planning with tax-saving benefits and moderate returns.

Fixed Deposit (FD)

Interest Rate: 5-7%

Lock-in Period: 5 Years

Exemption: Section 80C

Description: Tax-saving FDs offer a safe investment option with a fixed rate of return and a lock-in period of 5 years.
